About the Contest

This contest aims to showcase the best website created within a limited timeframe. The winner will be announced in the next theory class.

Rules and Guidelines

  • All participants must submit their websites within 48 hours.
  • The website should include header tags, paragraph tags, text formatting tags, images, lists, comments, audio, tables, and iframes.
  • Participants are encouraged to be creative and innovative in their designs.
  • No use of any GPTS
